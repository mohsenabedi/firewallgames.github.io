About the Game

DeadWave is an intense Unreal Engine 5 multiplayer shooter featuring diverse game modes for every playstyle. Battle against friends in competitive PvP modes like Team Deathmatch and Capture the Flag, test your stealth in the asymmetric Prop Hunt mode, or team up with friends to survive relentless waves of zombies in the cooperative Zombie Survival mode.

Built with Epic Online Services for seamless cross-platform multiplayer, DeadWave delivers fast-paced action, strategic gameplay, and endless replayability across multiple game modes.

About the Developer

Firewall Development is currently a solo independent game developer focused on creating unique and engaging gaming experiences. I am passionate about blending rich narratives with innovative gameplay mechanics, multiplayer games, and FPS and TPS shooters.
